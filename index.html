1. Recent CSS Feature: New viewport Units

It is very important to take into account the size of the display when developign a web page. The Viewport units enable the developer to ensure that the web page takes into account the size of the client's display as well as an existing toolbar.

  • BLUE Arrow: Indicates the height of the page. Does not take into account the toolbar
  • RED Arrow: Indicates the height visible on the screen due to the toolbar also taking up space

This issue can be dealt with using the new Viewport Units listed below:

  • Small viewport (svh/svw) : takes the address bar and the toolbar into consideration.
  • Large viewport (lvh/lvw) : doesn't take the address bar and the toolbar into consideration.
  • Dynamic viewport (dvh/dvw) : adapts its value when the toolbars are visible or/and when they are not.
*Important note: These units do not take into account the scroll bar. Therefore the used is situational and another method may be more suitable depending on the circumstances.

3. How AI is impacting the Web Design & Development

The automation and suggestions provided by AI can definitely be an asset when it comes to web design and development. The use of AI is overall positive, as it provides suggestions and is capable of recognizing patterns and analyzing on its own to provide results within seconds. It is however, very important not to rely on AI and always check due to the possibility of mistakes. AI can be prone to mistakes and should be used as a tool to help but not be relied on to get the entire work complete.
